SCell Office Component

Embedded, full control, customizable spreadsheet processor for your local solution or Cloud service.

You can easily integrate SCell in your ERP system or any digital document flow. SCell can take custom Data Reports, or you can directly set data in spreadsheet on the fly. With SCell you can achieve: report editing, flexible calculation on client side, flexible data management and print forms.
